Who We Are

Blood Buddies is for public donation center with blood donation members in the changing health care system.

  • Specialist blood donors and clinical supervision.
  • Increasing communication with our members.
  • High quality assessment, diagnosis and treatment.
  • Examine critically to ensure alignment.
  • The extra care of a multi-disciplinary team.

Do's and Dont's

  • Drink Plenty Of Water.
  • Eat A Good Meal.
  • Get Plenty Of Sleep The Night Before You Plan To Donate
  • Check To See If Any Medications You Are Taking Or Recently Took Would Prevent You From Donating
  • Avoid Blood Donation If You Are Not Feeling Well.
  • Avoid Smoking Or Drinking Before Donating Blood.
  • Avoid Donating Blood Without Eating i.e. On An Empty Stomach.
  • Avoid Blood Donation If You Are Having Tattoo.
